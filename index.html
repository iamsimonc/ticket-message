Spoken Word｜Feet on the Ground, Eyes Up

Keep your feet on the ground,
but don’t forget to look up.
You walk this road,
but you are not unseen.
Every step on earth
echoes somewhere higher.
Trust your pace —
Someone walks with you.
You’re not just moving forward,
you’re being guided.
Small steps still count,
heaven notices the quiet ones.
You don’t need to escape this world
to be held beyond it.
Even here,
light finds you.
You stand on solid ground,
but hope comes from above.
This path feels ordinary —
it’s not walked alone.
You carry questions,
and still you are carried.
The sky isn’t far,
it’s closer than you think.
Every honest step
leans toward grace.
You don’t have to climb to be reached.
Not just a journey on earth —
a calling unfolding.
When you pause,
the light does not leave.
You’re learning to walk,
not proving your worth.
Faith isn’t flying away —
it’s standing firm and looking up.
One brave step,
met by unseen hands.
You’re not lost —
you’re being led.
The ground feels heavy,
but the promise is light.
You’re allowed to struggle
and still be chosen.
Heaven doesn’t rush you.
Even here,
mercy is working quietly.
You don’t walk toward perfection,
you walk toward love.
Every morning
is a gentle call.
Your story is earthly,
but it leans eternal.
You are becoming —
watched with kindness.
One ticket,
more than this world can offer.
This life is real,
and it points higher.
You’re not asked to escape,
only to trust.
The road teaches your feet,
the sky teaches your heart.
You are seen
even when unnoticed.
Each step whispers,
“You are not alone.”
Courage grows
when you look up.
You don’t have to understand
to be held.
The call is gentle,
not forced.
You walk this world
with heaven in view.
Even now,
something good is guiding you.
Keep going —
grace keeps pace.
You’re allowed to live fully here
and hope beyond here.
The ground may shake,
but the promise stands.
Every step forward
leans into light.
You’re not climbing to earn love.
This is not just survival —
it’s becoming.
Heaven doesn’t pull you away,
it lifts you up.
You walk with questions,
and still you’re called.
The journey is human,
the hope is divine.
Stay grounded.
Stay open.
Final call —
walk boldly, look upward.
